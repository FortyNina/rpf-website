ABOUT THE GAME

Red Planet Farming is an educational strategy game that puts players in the shoes of the first Agricultural Director on Mars. Equipped with futuristic technology, players must grow enough food to feed and sustain a Martian colonyâ€”a task that pits them against dust storms, radiation, and other harsh climate conditions on Mars.

With Red Planet Farming, we aim to give players a better understanding of not only Mars, but the technology that will be used to one day sustain life there. The game familiarizes its players with common hazards and obstacles that future generations could face on Mars, like dust storms, radiation, and even Marsquakes. It also gives players a general understanding of futuristic tech like high-powered domes, grow lights, and radiation covers. Ultimately we want to get people excited about the next stages of space travel and the thrilling aspect of brining life to another planet.

Red Planet Farming has been in development since January 2018 and is the recipient of the 2019 NYU & Sloan Foundation Games Production grant.

ABOUT THE TEAM

Nina Demirjian is the lead developer and designer for Red Planet Farming. She recently graduated from New York University with a degree in Computer Science, and now works part-time as a developer at the American Museum of Natural History. She has been working on Red Planet Farming since January 2018.
